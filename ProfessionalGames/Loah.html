ABOUT LIFE OF A HUSTLER
Life of a Hustler is a fast-paced, work-in-progress arcade game inspired by the bold mindset behind the Life of a Hustler brand. It puts players in the shoes of an up-and-coming entrepreneur fighting their way to the top, while the world tries to push them back down. Run a high-pressure shop, serve customers at speed, manage stock levels, balance finances, and survive a constant stream of bills and setbacks. The odds are stacked against you, but that’s the point — hustlers don’t fold. They adapt, grind, and flip the script. Through its gameplay loop, Life of a Hustler highlights the grind, the obstacles, and the small wins that eventually turn into big victories. Your goal isn’t just to stay afloat — it’s to rise. Turn pressure into progress, chaos into cash flow, and earn your place at the top. Check out the brand and learn more about Life of a Hustler on their official website: https://lifeofahustler.co.uk.
STATE OF THE GAME
I began working with the Life of a Hustler brand in january. As the sole freelance developer, i have the role of programming the game, developing 3D assets, and creating promotional material. Utilising my own core tech systems i have been able to rapidly prototype the game. The game is currently in its alpha stages, going through a rigorous playtesting approach inspired by the methodology of Valve.

ABOUT DRIFT RACER
Drift racer is a fast paced racing game focused on high speeds and drifting mechanics. Players can choose from a variety of cars, and a variety of tracks, each with their own unique challenges and layouts. The game is focused on being arcade oriented with each track designed to take no longer than 1 minute to complete. The game boasts a vibrant cel-shaded art style with bright colours, an effective use of shaders, and a low poly art style for greater contrast.
MEDIA
WHY DRIFT RACER EXISTS
Drift racer was created as a part of my university coursework, specifically for a module focused on producing software within a team, and pitching said software to investors. The game was developed over a period of 6 weeks, with a team of 4 people including myself. While the main objective of the assignment was simply to create and present a presentation, we decided to go the extra mile and produce a polished game that could be added to our portfolios.
MY CONTRIBUTIONS
I worked as the project lead and primary developer on Drift Racer. Within my group i was the only person with any experience developing games and therfore acted as a teacher to my team members. I was responsible for creating the core drive mechanics including the drifting system, car physics, and track design. Additionally i handled the art direction ensuring a coheasive visual style accross the game. I also managed the project timeline, delegated tasks to team members, and ensured effective communication within the team to meet deadlines and deliver a polished final product.
MY PROUDEST ADDITIONS
As the main 3d artist on the project, I'm most proud of my shader graph work, alongside my map design and modelling. The shader graph work involved creating a lava shader that effectively simulated the look of molten lava, alongside this the overall game design was something i took great pride in, ensuring each track was fun, engaging, and flowed smoothly for players.
